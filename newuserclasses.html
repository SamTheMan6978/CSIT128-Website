EcoFitness is a state-of-the-art fitness facility located for students of the University of Wollongong.

With its modern equipment and dedicated staff, the EcoFitness provides an excellent environment for students, faculty, and staff to prioritize their health and fitness goals.
The gym offers a wide range of fitness classes, including yoga, aerobics, strength training, and spin classes, catering to individuals of all fitness levels.

Additionally, the EcoFitness provides personal training services for those seeking individualized guidance and support. With its spacious workout areas,
cardio machines, weightlifting equipment, and indoor track, the EcoFitness offers a comprehensive fitness experience.

Whether you are a beginner or an experienced fitness enthusiast, the EcoFitness is the perfect place to stay active and maintain a healthy lifestyle on campus.
